Tech Tips & Updates

Free advice to keep your devices running

Back up before you break down

Photos and contacts are irreplaceable. Turn on iCloud or Google backup today — before a drop or dead battery costs you.

Save your battery

Keep your charge between 20–80% and skip cheap chargers. It can add a year or more to your battery’s life.

Water damage? Act fast

Power it off, don’t charge it, and bring it in quickly. Rice is a myth — professional drying saves far more phones.

Slow phone or computer?

Usually it’s storage, updates, or malware — not “time for a new one.” A tune-up is often cheaper than you’d think.
